# Digieye Internship Projects

These projects were developed during my internship at **[Digieye](https://digieye.gr/)**, a web development company based in Thessaloniki, Greece (Jun 2025 – Aug 2025).

## About the Internship
During my internship, I worked as a **Web Developer Intern**, focusing on:
- Developing and maintaining websites and e-shops using **WordPress** and **WooCommerce**.
- Managing products, payments, and shipping integrations.
- Ensuring accessibility and responsive design.
- Communicating with clients to capture their needs and visual preferences.

---

## Projects
All the following projects were developed during my internship at Digieye using **WordPress**, **Elementor**, and **WooCommerce**.  
My main responsibilities included website design, product setup, SEO optimization, banking and shipping integrations, and accessibility improvements (WCAG).

### [Koukouvakids e-Shop](https://koukouvakids.gr/)
This was one of the first e-shops I worked on during my internship.
I was responsible for the full design of the website using WordPress and Elementor, including creating and organizing products in WooCommerce.
I also handled the integration of the banking system and shipping options, ensuring everything worked smoothly.
A key focus was on making the site fully responsive, accessible, and optimized for SEO, while maintaining a fun and family-friendly look for the brand.


### [Redboxstyle e-Shop](https://redboxstyle.gr/)
For this fashion e-shop, I created the overall structure and design in Elementor, keeping the layout modern and clean.
I managed the product setup and checkout flow, configured banking and shipping integrations, and worked on SEO improvements.
The goal was to create a visually appealing and fast-loading website that matched the style and personality of the brand.


### [Choumelia e-Shop](https://choumelia.com/)
This project involved creating an e-shop for traditional Greek products with an elegant and natural feel.
I developed the site with WordPress and Elementor, built the WooCommerce store, and set up banking and shipping connections.
I also paid special attention to accessibility and responsive design, ensuring users could easily navigate and shop from any device.
Additionally, I worked on SEO and product categorization for a smoother browsing experience.


### [Angelic Lashes e-Shop](https://angeliclashes.com/)
For this beauty e-shop, I was responsible for both design and functionality.
Using Elementor and WordPress, I created a clean, feminine design that highlights the brand’s services and products.
I implemented WooCommerce, integrated payment and delivery systems, and worked on SEO.


### [Kokkalis Law Firm Website](https://kokkalislaw.gr/)
This was a professional corporate website for a law firm.
I designed and structured the site using WordPress and Elementor, focusing on clarity and professionalism.
I ensured that the website was responsive and optimized for SEO, while keeping a simple layout that communicates trust and credibility.
